" DreamScape "

A Unity 3D puzzle game created with C#, players navigate through stylized dream levels.
Each level presents unique mechanics, including time manipulation, object scaling, and gravity manipulation to solve puzzles.
The game's narrative follows Dr. Mumble, a therapist who invents a dream-controlling device to aid in treating depression and anxiety. When tested on Luna, the device malfunctions, trapping her inside the dream .
Luna must go through challenging dreamscapes to secure her escape.
Ongoing PFE project in collaboration with my classmate, Hichri Chourouk.

" FattyBird "
